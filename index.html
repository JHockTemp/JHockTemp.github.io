Dr. Leroy L. Long

Leroy L. Long III, PhD is a husband, youth ministry leader, mentor, educator, artist, and dreamer from Dayton, OH. - the birth place of aviation, the cash register, and Funk. He is a proud graduate of Dayton Public Schools and Wright STEPP-Wright State University's Science, Technology, and Engineering Preparatory Program (STEPP).

Check my book "Poetic Justness"

By day, he teaches engineering classes, conducts educational research, and volunteers in the community as a college professor. In his spare time, he enjoys watching and playing sports, competing in card/domino games, exercising, reading, writing poetry and traveling with his wife.
