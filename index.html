Leroy Long III

"Lead with love, follow up with justice!"

Leroy Long III, PhD is a husband, youth ministry leader, mentor, educator, artist, author and dreamer. He is from Dayton, OH - the birthplace of aviation, the cash register, Funk and the NFL's first game. He is a proud graduate of Dayton Public Schools and Wright STEPP - Wright State University's Science, Technology, and Engineering Preparatory Program.

» Check out his book Poetic Justness via Blurb, Amazon, Google, or Barnes and Noble.

By day, he teaches engineering classes, conducts educational research, and volunteers in the community as a college professor. In his spare time, he enjoys watching and playing sports, competing in card and domino games, exercising, reading, writing poetry and traveling with his wife.
